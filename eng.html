About Us

NeoCrime is a Ukrainian Virtual Trucking Company founded on January 8, 2026, by its owner F4DED. Despite being a relatively new company, NeoCrime has been developing rapidly and continues to grow its community of passionate virtual drivers.

The company brings together players who share a passion for trucking, teamwork, and exploring the roads of Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator in multiplayer through TruckersMP.

NeoCrime actively participates in the TruckersMP community. Our drivers regularly attend public convoys and large-scale events, proudly representing the company and building connections within the community.

One of the core values of NeoCrime is maintaining a friendly, supportive, and active community. We welcome drivers of all experience levels, from beginners to experienced players, providing a place where everyone can feel part of a team.

CrimeADMIN

CrimeADMIN is our pride and unique development. A multifunctional Discord bot created specifically to fully automate and manage all processes within the NeoCrime virtual company.

The bot features its own complex economy (CrimeCoins and currency) and an EXP level system. Our drivers earn currency by participating in convoys, passing qualification tests, opening truck cases, and through daily activity.

CrimeADMIN handles all the routine: it automatically processes applications from new candidates, manages the report system, issues salaries, and provides access to an interactive fleet where vehicles are distributed by class and driver experience.

On weekends, the bot automatically launches the global Road Kings Weekend event, tracking voice channel activity and dropping "Secret Cases". And thanks to a reliable Anti-Raid system, our server always remains safe!
