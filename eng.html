About Us

NeoCrime is not just a virtual trucking company in Euro Truck Simulator 2. It is a united Ukrainian community where every driver is an essential part of a single team. We bring together people inspired by the road, co-op gaming, and mutual support.

We offer a friendly and active community, regular convoys and joint trips, an atmosphere of mutual respect, and discipline on the road. NeoCrime values positive communication, zero toxicity, and a genuine passion for the game.

Our values are built on respect, teamwork, and a quality approach to transportation. Every member matters to us, regardless of their experience or playstyle. Join NeoCrime and become a part of the Ukrainian drivers' community in ETS2 MP.

🚛 CONVOY EVENT «RACING FOR DLC» — NEOCRIME VTC

20.03.2026 | Author: !F4DED

On March 19, 2026, the virtual trucking company NeoCrime hosted a unique convoy event in a racing format with prizes, delivering an exciting and adrenaline-filled experience for all participants 🚛🔥

This time, drivers didn’t just cruise — they competed for victory while transporting a challenging cargo: 21 tons of fuel. Such a load required maximum concentration, as the truck could easily lose stability and overturn at any moment.

🏆 Race Results:

  • 🥇 1st place — prize: American Truck Simulator (ATS)
  • 🥈 2nd place — prize: American Truck Simulator (ATS)
  • 🥉 3rd place — prize: DLC for Euro Truck Simulator 2 (of choice)

Despite the difficult route and dangerous cargo, all participants successfully reached the finish line, demonstrating a high level of driving skill, discipline, and teamwork.

The event took place in a friendly atmosphere — everyone enjoyed the experience, and the race once again proved that NeoCrime is not just a VTC, but a true community of like-minded drivers 💛

🤝 OFFICIAL NEWS ABOUT THE PARTNERSHIP VTC NEOCRIME × TWISTED TRANSPORT

10.03.2026 | Author: !F4DED

We are pleased to announce great news for the entire TruckersMP community — the Ukrainian virtual trucking company NeoCrime has officially partnered with the large English VTC Twisted Transport.

Twisted Transport is one of the well-known and Validated VTCs in TruckersMP, founded in March 2022. The company is actively developing within the community and unites over 150+ members from all over the world, regularly organizing convoys, events, and maintaining a friendly atmosphere among drivers.

The partnership between NeoCrime and Twisted Transport opens up new opportunities for both companies:

  • 🔹 joint participation in convoys and major events;
  • 🔹 mutual support of events;
  • 🔹 expanding international connections within the TruckersMP community;
  • 🔹 exchange of experience between teams and members.

We are confident that this collaboration will be an important step in the development of both VTCs and will help create even more interesting and large-scale events for the entire community.

🔗 Twisted Transport on TruckersMP:
https://truckersmp.com/vtc/53248

💬 Twisted Transport Discord server:
https://discord.gg/jpfEJ2ZhcD

NeoCrime continues to actively develop and expand its network of partners, and we are excited to start a new chapter of cooperation together with Twisted Transport.

🚗 JOINT CONVOY ON SKODA

06.03.2026 | Author: !F4DED

The NeoCrime team took part in a joint convoy using Skoda cars together with Ukraine Community Logistics and Fast Line UA. The event took place in a friendly atmosphere and brought participants together for an enjoyable and well-organized drive.

🌎 NEOCRIME EXPANDS: NOW IN AMERICAN TRUCK SIMULATOR

The community NeoCrime, originally founded as a company for fans of Euro Truck Simulator 2, officially announces a new stage of development. We are actively expanding our activities beyond Europe — NeoCrime is now represented in American Truck Simulator as well.

Since our foundation, we have been steadily growing within ETS2: organizing regular convoys, participating in large-scale events, and bringing together drivers from different parts of the world. Today, we are taking another step forward by launching a full ATS division.

  • 🔹 We are developing our structure further and recruiting new drivers specifically for the ATS division.

Our goal remains the same — to build an active, friendly, and well-organized community for all trucking enthusiasts. NeoCrime is not just a VTC; it's a team that continuously evolves and opens new opportunities for its members.

More events, more shared journeys, and even bigger convoys are ahead!

🚀 TECHNOLOGICAL BREAKTHROUGH IN NEOCRIME: FULL AUTOMATION!

12.02.2026 | Author: !F4DED

The NeoCrime team is excited to announce a major milestone in our company's journey! We have successfully developed and implemented our own automated system, fully integrated with the TruckersHub platform!

Thanks to the hard work of our developers, we have moved away from outdated tracking methods. Now, all driver statistics and activities are tracked in real-time.

What does this change?

  • 🔹 Full automation: the system automatically logs mileage and member achievements.
  • 🔹 Career growth: no more manual reports — rank promotions happen automatically upon reaching new milestones.
  • 🔹 Transparency and comfort: we have created an environment where drivers can focus solely on the road and enjoying the game.

For us, this is not just a technical update, but a step towards building a modern, convenient, and professional community. NeoCrime continues to evolve, introducing innovations for our drivers.

NeoCrime — technology driving us forward!
