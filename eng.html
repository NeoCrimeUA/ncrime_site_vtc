About Us

NeoCrime is not just a virtual trucking company in Euro Truck Simulator 2. It is a united Ukrainian community where every driver is an essential part of a single team. We bring together people inspired by the road, co-op gaming, and mutual support.

We offer a friendly and active community, regular convoys and joint trips, an atmosphere of mutual respect, and discipline on the road. NeoCrime values positive communication, zero toxicity, and a genuine passion for the game.

Our values are built on respect, teamwork, and a quality approach to transportation. Every member matters to us, regardless of their experience or playstyle. Join NeoCrime and become a part of the Ukrainian drivers' community in ETS2 MP.
