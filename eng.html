About Us

NeoCrime is an active and friendly community that professionally explores the roads of ETS2 and ATS. We focus on active participation in the TruckersMP community life, attending numerous public events every week, as well as running our own popular convoys once every two weeks.

Our Benefits

What makes the NeoCrime community special

01

Friendly Community

We take pride in our warm atmosphere! At NeoCrime, every driver will find support, help, and great company for joint trips. Become part of our close-knit team on the virtual roads!

02

Experienced Staff

Our team of administrators is always ready to help. We ensure a comfortable game experience, quickly resolve any issues, and create all the conditions for your growth in the world of virtual trucking.

03

Regular Convoys

We regularly hold joint trips in ETS2 and ATS. This is the perfect opportunity to enjoy scenic routes in a convoy, improve your teamwork driving skills, and have a great time together!

04

Unique Events

NeoCrime regularly organizes unconventional events, from group road trips to extreme truck races. Take part, show your skills, and win valuable prizes and DLC!
