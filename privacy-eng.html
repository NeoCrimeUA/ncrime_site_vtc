Privacy

How we handle your data

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This document explains how the virtual trucking company NeoCrime (hereinafter — "we") handles your information. We respect the privacy of our members and strive to ensure maximum transparency in data processing.

2. What We Learn About You

We do not collect data automatically through the website. The only way we receive information is through your voluntary submission of application forms (for example, via Google Forms) to join NeoCrime. This usually includes:

  • Your in-game nickname and a link to your TruckersMP/Steam profile.
  • Age (to comply with the community's age limits).
  • Any other information you choose to share during the interview.

3. Why We Need This Data

The information is used exclusively for the company's internal coordination:

  • Reviewing your application for the driver role.
  • Organizing joint convoys and events.
  • Contacting you via Discord or email.

4. Security and Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell or share your nicknames or contacts with advertising agencies or third parties. Only NeoCrime administrators have access to your responses. Since we use Google services, your information is also protected according to Google's global security standards.

5. Your Rights

You are the owner of your data. At any time, you can contact us to find out what we store, or ask us to completely delete your application from our database.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, write to us:

  • Email: neocrime10@gmail.com
  • Discord: NeoCrime Server
