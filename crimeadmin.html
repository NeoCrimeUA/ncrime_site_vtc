About the system CrimeADMIN

CrimeADMIN is the pride and unique development of NeoCrime. A multifunctional Discord bot created specifically for full automation and management of all processes within the virtual company.

Economy & Levels

Own currency CrimeCoin and experience (EXP) system for activity, convoys, and tests.

Full Automation

Application processing, complaint management, and automatic salary payouts to drivers.

Interactive Fleet

Access to vehicles categorized by class and driver experience.

Security & Events

Anti-Raid system and launching the Road Kings Weekend event with "Secret Cases".

The bot is constantly updated, adding new features for your comfort in NeoCrime.

Bot Features

Economy

  • /work Get salary and EXP (once per 24 hours). The longer the daily streak, the higher the bonus.
  • /balance Check account balance (CrimeCoin, Bonuses, EXP).
  • /pay Transfer funds between players (10% bank fee).

Case System

  • /case_menu Open cases (Daily, Regular, Unique, Secret).
  • Cases drop unique trucks for your personal fleet or large amounts of CrimeCoins.

Statistics

  • /stats Your profile: level, number of convoys, days in the company.
  • /top Top 10 richest and highest level leaders.
  • /top_activity Rating by convoys, donations, and work shifts.

NeoCrime Casino

  • /slot Slot machine (bet up to 500 CC).
  • /dice Dice roll for even/odd.
  • /roulette Roulette (Red, Black, Green).

Testing

  • /quiz Test on traffic rules, ETS2/ATS, and company charter (once per hour).
  • A correct answer gives CrimeCoin and EXP, a wrong one - a fine.

Social Life

  • /birthday Set your birthday.
  • /birthdays View the company holiday calendar.
  • Automatic counting in a separate channel with its own leaderboard.

Automation — Our Choice

We left the routine in the past. CrimeADMIN takes care of all the bureaucracy, allowing management to focus on company development, and drivers — on the road.

VTC Management

Dynamic nickname changing with position tags, automatic access granting, and real-time public team list updating.

Smart Applications

One-click application processing. The bot automatically removes old roles, assigns new ones, formats the nickname, and creates a log for instructors.

Anti-Raid Shield

Built-in neuro-guard. The bot continuously analyzes server activity in the background. If it detects any sabotage attempts or security threats, it instantly and independently neutralizes the violator.

Road Kings Tracker

On weekends, the system independently tracks your activity in voice channels, quietly crediting salary and EXP every hour.

CrimeADMIN Jr.

The utility module of the system. Its element is logistics, continuous monitoring, and full synchronization with spreadsheets.

Convoy Automation

Logisticians just need to enter data into a Google Sheet. The bot will extract the information, create a beautiful announcement, and publish it on schedule.

Chain Queue

If several trips are scheduled for a day, the system builds them into a smart queue and publishes them sequentially one after another.

Voice Gatherings and Telegram

Automatic creation of gathering notifications in the voice channel and instant duplication of routes to the corporate Telegram.

CrimeADMIN Jr.

TruckersHub API

The bot is directly connected to the TruckersHub database, allowing for full automation of HR management and the hosting of company global events.

Auto-Rank System

Tracks mileage and automatically promotes drivers (from Trainee to Veteran), independently assigning roles in Discord and sending congratulations.

Road Kings Tracking

From Friday to Monday, the system quietly 'takes a snapshot' of each participant's starting mileage to begin the global event.

Road Monetization

Every kilometer driven on weekends is converted into CrimeCoin. The bot automatically calculates the salary in PMs and forms the TOP-10 best drivers.
