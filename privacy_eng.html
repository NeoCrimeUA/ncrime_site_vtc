Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This document explains how the virtual trucking company NeoCrime (hereinafter referred to as "we") handles your information. We respect the privacy of our members and strive to ensure maximum transparency in data processing.

2. What information we collect about you

We do not collect data automatically through the website. The only way we receive information is through your voluntary submission of application forms (e.g., via Google Forms) to join NeoCrime. Typically, this includes:

  • Your in-game nickname and link to your TruckersMP/Steam profile.
  • Age (to comply with the community's age limits).
  • Any other information you choose to provide during the interview.

3. Why we need this data

The information is used exclusively for the internal coordination of the company:

  • Reviewing your application for a driver position.
  • Organizing joint convoys and events.
  • Communicating with you via Discord or email.

4. Security and sharing with third parties

We do not sell or share your nicknames or contact details with advertising agencies or third parties. Only NeoCrime administrators have access to your responses. Since we use Google services, your information is also protected according to Google's global security standards.

5. Your rights

You are the owner of your data. At any time, you can contact us to find out what information we store or request the complete deletion of your application from our database.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy, please contact us:

  • Email: neocrime10@gmail.com
  • Discord: NeoCrime Server
